I am a mathematics PhD student at Penn State specializing in dynamics (or, using somewhat older but more descriptive terminology, "the qualitative theory of dynamical systems"). Currently my focus is on measure rigidity of smooth higher rank abelian actions. My thesis adviser is Prof. Federico Rodriguez Hertz.

I hold a B.Sc. in mathematics and B.A. in philosophy from Koc University in Istanbul, Turkey. My philosophical studies are currently dormant but broadly speaking I am interested in (Husserl-adjacent) phenomenology.
